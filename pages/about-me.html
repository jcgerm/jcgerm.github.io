About Me

About 8 years ago, I picked up a guitar for the first time. Okay, that's not entirely true. Like a lot of other people, I already had a guitar. It just took up most of the space under my bed at the time. I could play a few chords, and maybe a couple songs. But, I didn't really get into guitar playing until about 8 years ago when I started to listening to the Jeff Buckley Live at Sin-é album.
